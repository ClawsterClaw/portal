Capabilities

Five standalone phases. Use any one independently — no forced sequencing. Each applies across all domains.

① RESEARCH

Research

Deep exploration of any topic. Structure findings into context, key signals, implications and open questions. Works across all domains — markets, AI, science, disruptive innovation, human evolution, geopolitics and beyond.

stocks AI crypto science disruptive innovation human evolution with AI futures
Standalone — start here without committing to any next step.
② PLAN

Plan

Turn any idea, research finding or goal into a structured, executable plan. Covers business strategy, product roadmaps, trading frameworks, content strategies, marketing plans and more.

business strategy product roadmap marketing plan trading framework content strategy
Standalone — plan without having done a formal Research phase first.
③ EXECUTE

Execute

Deliver on a plan or idea. Build a website, write a report, develop an app, create marketing content, write a blog post, analyse a trade, send a message. Development is one tool under execution — not the only one.

development writing marketing SEO content analysis trading tools
Standalone — build or create without a formal Plan phase.
④ REVIEW

Review

Verify quality and raise standards on anything delivered. Review and test code before go-live, improve a document, critique a strategy, stress-test a thesis, refine copy before publishing.

code review document review strategy critique copy editing thesis stress-test
Standalone — review anything at any point, not just after Execute.
⑤ COMPOUND

Compound

Capture what worked, what didn't, and why. For marketing — what drove results. For trading — what the setup taught you. For research — what the key insight was. Feeds back into every future session automatically.

learnings patterns decisions improvements
The most important phase. The longer you use it, the smarter every other phase becomes.
